About Me


I'm Alex Eng, a Malaysian currently based in Toronto.
Completed my B.Sc in Computer Science at the University of Guelph as of May 2019.

The aspects I've most enjoyed from programming is emphathizing with users on their obstacles and experiences. With them in mind, I seek practical solutions via programming, visuals, psychology, and other expertise available.
I mainly work with Python, with side experiences in C, Java, PostgreSQL. For the frontend, it would be HTML, and CSS while utilizing Bootstrap for responsive web development. I also made some web and android games through Unity and C# for project Blue Kuma Games.

Aside programming, my hobbies are drawing, cooking, gaming, and occasionally gymming. You may find some art, food, and photography samples under the Graphics section.
